HACKERFEST

"Join our 24-hour hackfest where innovators, developers, and designers come together to solve real-world problems. Collaborate, create, and compete to build innovative solutions using the latest technologies. Enjoy mentorship from industry experts, prizes for outstanding projects, and networking opportunities with like-minded individuals. Showcase your skills, learn from others, and take your ideas to the next level! Our hackfest is the perfect platform to transform your ideas into reality, make a difference, and have fun while doing it. So, gather your team, register now, and get ready to hack, learn, and grow!"

SPONSERSHIP

"Erode Sengunthar Engineering College (ESEC) is a premier institution located in Erode, Tamil Nadu, dedicated to providing quality education in the field of engineering and technology. Established in 1996, ESEC is affiliated with Anna University and approved by AICTE, New Delhi. The college is recognized for its commitment to excellence in education, research, and innovation."
